Who am I?

Only a tiny spec in this vast universe! Who is also a pitbull lover, language enthusiast, and now, also a baby coder. My pup Marley and I are living in Portland, as I code my way into the tech world.
It wasn't until a bit over 2 years ago that I had a revelation: I could make a career out of my love for tech! Weird, I know. As a 26 year old, I felt like I had already reached my potential, making my way through various desk jobs in the human services world. Technology was never presented to me as a viable career path growing up. I worked full time while I finished high school and got my associates degree in community college. By the age of 20, I was working for a public agency until I moved to Portland and realized my love for technology doesn't have to be just a hobby. I decided to go back to school and began working on my Computer Engineering degree Spring of '16 at PSU. Although, I am currently taking a small hiatus from PSU and joined Alchemy Code Lab's Coding Bootcamp!
